About

Snaky is a simple JQuery component used to render html elements in a S or snake sort of path, going left to right for the even rows (starting at 0) and then right to left for the odd rows. Like this:

-----------------
| 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 |
-----------------
| 8 | 7 | 6 | 5 |
-----------------
